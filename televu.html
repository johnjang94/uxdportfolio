Context & Background

TeleVU Innovations Inc. is a privately-owned telecommunication corporation that connects the clinicians and the patients on a remote-basis to reduce the wait times and to offer improved medical assistance.

TeleVU Innovations Inc. offers two different products that are connected to each other. One of them is the access to the desktop website that clinicians can use to treat patients remotely.

On Desktop website, the customers can sign-up, cancel, call another customer who use the same program, initiate a video-call, record a video-call (while in session) or review the recorded videos in history.


TeleVU Website Portal

The other product is the service in glasses which is wearable. This machine is usually provided to the guardians or nurses who can assist the patients.

Similar to the Desktop website, the patient's guardian(s) or the nurse-in-charge can either dial or accept the call from clinicians who are calling them from the Desktop website.
