Welcome!

Welcome to my mini game project.

On this web page, you will play a guessing game either just by yourself or with your friends.

Please read the following rules:

    Rules

  1. You can only guess a random number between 1 and 100
  2. You will be given a total of 5 chances to guess the number that the game system has generated
    (No, you will not know the correct number until you've guessed it correctly)

  3. You can only guess the whole number. No decimals are accepted

      ** Accepted numbers:
    • 34 ✅
    • 30 ✅
    • 27 ✅

      ** Not accepted numbers:
    • 34.2 ❌
    • 35.8 ❌
    • 12.5 ❌

  4. Your chance will be deducted every time you guess incorrectly. Once your chance reaches at 0, you will not be able to play the game anymore.

Please remember the following conditions as well:

  • If you try to guess any number below 1 or above 100, you will be reminded to guess a random number within the range.
  • If you try to guess any number with decimals, you will be reminded to guess with whole numbers.
  • If you try to guess the same number twice, you will be reminded to try a different number.
  • Your chance will not be deducted if you make above attempts.
